Game Instructions

  • Press R to Start the game.
  • Press P to Pause / Resume.
  • Use Arrow Keys for movement.
  • Short press Space = Periodic Bullet Fire.
  • Long press Space = Rapid Bullet Fire.
  • Meteors require 5 hits.
  • Small enemies die in 1 hit.
  • Big enemies require 10 hits.
  • Every big enemy destroyed spawns 2 small enemies.
  • After destroying 10 big enemies, the Boss appears.
  • The Boss requires 20 hits.

Mobile Controls

  • Touch directional buttons to move.
  • Hold the FIRE button to shoot.
  • Tap START to begin the game.
  • Tap PAUSE to pause or resume.
  • Play in Landscape Mode for best performance.
