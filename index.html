Game Instructions

  • Press R to Start the game
  • Press P to Pause / Resume the game
  • Use Arrow Keys for movement
  • Short press Space = Periodic Bullet Fire
  • Long press Space = Rapid Bullet Fire
  • Meteors/Rocks: Shoot 5 times to destroy
  • Small Enemies: Die in 1 shot
  • Bigger/Brown Enemies: Shoot 10 times to destroy
  • Each big enemy killed spawns 2 more small enemies
  • After killing 10 big enemies, a Boss appears
  • Shoot the Boss 20 times to defeat it
